Isotherm Fitting

This module allows you to fit isotherm models to experimental adsorption data.

Mathematical Models

We use models such as Langmuir, Freundlich, and Sips isotherms. For example, the Langmuir isotherm is given by:

$$ q = \frac{q_\text{max} b C}{1 + b C} $$

where:

  • q: adsorbed amount
  • C: equilibrium concentration
  • q_max: maximum adsorption capacity
  • b: Langmuir constant

Usage

./fit_isotherm --input data.csv --model langmuir --output results.json
